About me

My name is Anna Nguyen, and I'm a third-year undergrad at Cornell University studying Information Science (UX/UI) and minoring in Computer Science.

My background lies in the intersection between design and technology, meaning I design experiences with an awareness for technical constraints and I'm comfortable communicating across development and design teams to solve problems.

I love connecting with people and hearing their stories, and hope to further explore user research/engineering, frontend development, visual design, and accessibility design. Using the skills I have, I hope to support underserved communities and to continuously listen, understand, serve, and empathize.

Currently, I am a product designer at Cornell Hack4Impact. I have also had the opportunity to work with several impactful organizations like the National Alliance for Volunteer Engagement (NAVE), Barefoot College, Aurolab, and currently Farmworker Justice. Outside of design, you can find me playing fashion diva, selling my soul to people's pet pictures, or DIY-ing bead daisy rings (hit me up if you want one).

Have a lovely day and thank you for visiting :)
