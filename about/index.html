Thank you for visiting :) My name is Anna Nguyen, and I'm a third-year undergrad at Cornell University studying computer science and information science (UX/UI). Originally a CS major, I stumbled into design after taking an Intro to Product Design class and fell in love with how versatile it could be. I found that I loved connecting with people and communities and hearing all their different stories, which inspires me to better inform my own decisions as a developer and designer and to continually strive to become a better ally. Using the skills I have, I hope to support underserved communties and to continuously listen, understand, and empathize.

Some fun facts about me: I love skincare & fashion. I make beaded flower rings in my free time (hit me up if you want one!!). And my great-grandma was supposedly a part of Vietnamese royalty.
